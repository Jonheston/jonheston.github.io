Latitude vs. Max Temp (f)



Observations


There is a quite obvious inverted-U function with max temp peaking at around the equator and declining in both directions. Interestingly there is an asymmetry with colder temperatures being recorded at in the extreme North, though I suspect this reflects the fact that this is a result of there being fewer cities in the extreme South and this affects the data collected.
